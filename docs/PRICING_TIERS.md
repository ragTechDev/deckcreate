### Our competitive edge

1. Bundled multi-format outputs, not isolated line items.
2. Lower all-in content cost when clients need episode + social repurposing every cycle.
3. Faster campaign velocity through one workflow for long-form, short-form, and carousel outputs.
4. Designed for recurring publishing systems and retainers, not one-off production transactions.
5. Better fit for teams that already have footage and need consistent post-production throughput.

### Why choose poddedit

1. You get one publishing engine per episode: long-form, short-form, and social assets under one operating rhythm.
2. Planning, edits, and output formats are aligned to repeat weekly or bi-weekly without rebuilding process each time.
3. Commercial predictability is clearer for long-term partnerships than ad hoc add-on stacking.
4. Delivery model is optimized for content teams that value consistency, speed, and format coverage.
